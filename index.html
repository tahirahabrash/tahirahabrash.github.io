Oh hey there!

Hello! My name is Tahirah Abrash.

I am a multidisciplinary designer based out of Toronto, Canada.

I graduated from Centennial College in 2013 with an Advanced Diploma in Broadcasting & Film. From there I took my love for design and typography in film and enrolled in the Digital Graphic Design certificate program at Seneca College in 2016.

I'm passionate about creating genuine experiences through digital and print media. From brand identity and logo design to print ads and web design, I solve problems through visual communication.
